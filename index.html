Frequently Asked Questions

A phenol skin peel is an advanced chemical peel that penetrates deeply into the skin to address significant aging concerns. It works by removing damaged skin cells and stimulating collagen production, resulting in dramatic improvement in wrinkles, age spots, and overall skin texture. The treatment is performed under medical supervision and provides long-lasting results.

Recovery typically takes 7-14 days, with initial healing occurring in the first week. You'll need to follow specific aftercare instructions and avoid sun exposure. Most patients can return to work after 2 weeks, with final results continuing to improve over 3-6 months.

Results from a phenol peel can last 10-15 years or more with proper skin care and sun protection. The treatment provides one of the longest-lasting results of any non-surgical skin rejuvenation procedure available.

The procedure is performed under appropriate anesthesia to ensure comfort. While you may experience some discomfort during recovery, we provide comprehensive pain management and detailed aftercare instructions to minimize any discomfort.

Ideal candidates are individuals with significant skin aging concerns who are in good overall health. A consultation with our specialists will determine if this treatment is right for you. The procedure is most effective for fair to medium skin tones and may not be suitable for very dark skin.
